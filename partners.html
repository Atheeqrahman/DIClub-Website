Events

Inception

March, 2019


Inception, the Design and Innovation Club's debut, showcased creative teams presenting innovative ideas, culminating in a standout winner from the finalists, setting a high bar for innovation.

Retrograde

March, 2019


Retrograde, part of the Design and Innovation Club's inaugural event on March 4, 2019, was a captivating quiz contest. Participants spotted peculiar inventions, coined names, and marketed them in their historical context.

Postagram

March, 2019


Postagram, hosted by the Design and Innovation Club, featured a digital poster design contest. Teams of two created posters online on March 1, 2019, for round one, with finalists competing in a remarkable on-campus final round on March 4, 2019.

Tech-Talkathon

October, 2019


Design and Innovation Club launched Tech-Talkathon on October 14, 2019, where participants shared tech insights with Major General A. Arun as the guest. The event's finale on October 15, 2019, crowned winners with exciting prizes.
